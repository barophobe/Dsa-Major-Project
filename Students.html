International Students


Unipupil is a unique One-Stop global education portal, connecting students and education institutions around the world.

With courses from institutions around the world, Unipupil is the place to find exactly the right course for you. Whether you’re finishing school soon and looking for the right degree, or looking to upskill to help with your career, Unipupil’s FREE portal makes taking the next step in your education simple




Search for, compare and book courses directly with the institution of your choice, using Unipupil’s secure payment method – you can even read reviews from current or former students to help you make your choice.


So, start using our free search function today. With Unipupil, the world of education is right at your fingertips.
