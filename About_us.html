About Unipupil


Unipupil is a unique One-Stop global education portal, connecting students and education institutions around the world.

Unipupils web portal is designed to help students search for, compare and book courses at over 140,000 institutions worldwide. Our detailed search function means students can search courses by type, location, price and rating. Students can then use our secure online payment system to book a course directly with the institution at no extra cost.


For Colleges, Universities and Schools, Unipupil is a FREE online platform to showcase institutions and courses on a global scale. The institution is totally in control of the content they wish to share through their own personalised profile page.


For education agents who play a key role in bringing international opportunities to the right students, Unipupil offers full facilities for them to sign up students and engage with a global network of educational institutions via the portal.


With Unipupil, the world of education is right at your fingertips.
